How It Works

From Symptom to Solution

Saarthi uses a trained neural network to process your input and deliver the right guidance in seconds.

1

Describe Your Condition

Type or speak your symptoms naturally. Saarthi uses SpaCy NLP to tokenize, lemmatize, and understand your input — no medical jargon needed.

2

AI Processes & Classifies

Your input is converted into a bag-of-words representation and fed through a trained Keras neural network that classifies it against 50+ known health conditions.

3

Receive Personalized Guidance

Saarthi delivers step-by-step first aid, medication info, lifestyle tips, or recommends connecting with a doctor — all in calm, reassuring language.

4

Connect with a Professional

If the situation requires professional attention, Saarthi helps you find and contact nearby doctors or emergency services immediately.
